Here are 4 suggested steps for getting started with Paco.

  1. 'Find Experiments' shows you available experiments to join.
    Click on an experiment to find out more and to join it.

  2. 'Current Experiments' manages the experiments you have joined.
    Press on an experiment to self report data.
    Long press on an experiment to modify it.

  3. Visualize Your Data
    Once you have collected some data, there are two ways to look at it:
    • LongPress on the experiment name in the Current Experiment list
    • Press "Explore Your Data" from the main screen.

  4. Create your own experiments!
    This is the real power of Paco.
    • On your PC, login to the server, https://quantifiedself.appspot.com/Main.html, with the whitelisted account you are using with Paco
    • Click "Administer Experiments/Create New"
    • Fill out the definition of the experiment
    • Save the experiment

    Back on your phone, press "Find Experiments" and press "Refresh Experiments" to download and join your new experiment.
